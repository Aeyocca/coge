Papaya genome in SynView

A new section was added explaining the use of the papaya genome in understanding the post-tetraploid evolution of the Arabidopsis genome. This also provides an overview of how SynView's three way comparison of genomic regions can help discover interesting patterns in genome evolution. Please note that access to the papaya genome is restricted to members of the papaya genome research consortium.
