How do I log into CoGe?
 
When you visit CoGe, you will be asked to log in.  If you have a user account, please enter your user name and password, then press "Log on".  If you don't have a user account, you don't have to enter anything and can just press "Use Public Login".
What is the different between a public and user login?
 
Some information in CoGe can't be released to the general public.  If you have a user account, you will want to use it to get access to information that might be otherwise restricted.  However, nearly all the information in CoGe is available to the public.
